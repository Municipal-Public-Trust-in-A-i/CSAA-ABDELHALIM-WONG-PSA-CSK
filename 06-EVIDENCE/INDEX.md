## The Core Evidence Suppression Scheme

The following is the documented sequence of the evidentiary scheme, based on the court record:

### Timeline

| Date | Event |
|---|---|
| 2021 | Collision between Plaintiff Rosario and Defendant Abdelhalim's vehicle |
| 21 days post-collision | CSAA issues denial letter claiming "concluded investigation" |
| Within 3 months of collision | Plaintiff's former counsel obtains 911 recording via public records request |
| Discovery period | Defense receives materials through discovery production |
| May 4, 2023 | Michael R. Chambers / Carbone, Smith & Koyama transfer defense file to Phillips, Spallas & Angstadt |
| February 18, 2025 | Pre-trial hearing: defense makes false representations re: provenance and receipt of evidence |
| April 2025 | Trial: defense continues false "non-receipt / unknown provenance" narrative |
| April 16-17, 2025 | Court denies continuances; excludes / defers admission of critical evidence |
| April 23, 2025 | 9-3 defense verdict; deliberating jury questions about causation |
