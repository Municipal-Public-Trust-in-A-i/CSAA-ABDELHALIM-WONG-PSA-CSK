### Key Deposition Testimony

**Nicholas Raffin (CSAA PMK):** Testimony regarding CSAA's investigative procedures and what
the insurer did and did not obtain before issuing the denial letter. Core evidence for
the 631802 insurance fraud action.

**Subhi Abdelhalim:** Defendant's sworn account of the collision, for comparison against
the 911 call admission (*"I barely hit him with anything"*).

**Officer David Fernandez:** Testimony regarding body-worn camera footage, incident response,
and observations at the scene.
